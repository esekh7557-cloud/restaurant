Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

15 years of crafting memories, one plate at a time.

Since 2010

From a Small Kitchen to Pune's Beloved Destination

Spice Route began as a dream — a modest 12-seater eatery in the heart of Koregaon Park, run by Chef Arjun Sharma and his family. Armed with generations-old recipes from the kitchens of Lucknow and a deep love for authentic flavors, he set out to create a dining experience that felt like home.

What started as a neighborhood secret quickly became the talk of the city. Word spread — not through advertisements, but through the irresistible aroma that wafted through the streets and the stories told by satisfied customers over countless meals.

Today, Spice Route seats over 120 guests, employs a team of 35 passionate food artisans, and has served more than 50,000 happy customers. But our core remains the same: honest food, made with love, served with warmth.

— Chef Arjun Sharma, Founder
Our Philosophy

What We Stand For

Every detail matters — from the spices we source to the smiles we serve.

🌾

Farm to Table

We source vegetables and spices directly from local farms in Maharashtra. Every ingredient is fresh, seasonal, and traceable — no shortcuts, no compromises.

🔥

Cooked with Soul

Our dishes are slow-cooked using traditional techniques — tandoor, dum, and charcoal grilling. We believe food made in a rush can never taste like food made with passion.

🛡️

Hygiene First

FSSAI certified kitchen with daily health inspections, UV-sterilized water, and staff health checks. We treat your health as our responsibility.

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

2010

The Beginning

Chef Arjun opens a 12-seater restaurant in Koregaon Park with his family's heirloom recipes.

2014

First 10,000 Customers

Crossed 10K customers milestone. Expanded to 60 seats and introduced the legendary Dum Biryani.

2018

Award Winning

Won "Best North Indian Restaurant in Pune" by Times Food Awards. Launched Swiggy & Zomato delivery.

2024

50,000+ Happy Customers

120+ seats, 200+ dishes, 4.6★ Google rating, and still growing — with the same love from Day 1.
